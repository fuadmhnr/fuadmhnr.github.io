Designing for attention starts with defending your own. This is a short field guide to protect focus without turning into a monk.

## The Rule of One Window

Keep one intentional window open: the one where the work happens. Every extra tab is a tiny promise you will break later.

## A Tiny Checklist

- What must exist when I stop today?
- What is the smallest version that proves the idea?
- What can I delete without guilt?
